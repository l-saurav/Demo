Bachelor of Computer Application

Affiliated to Pokhara University

BCA offered by the Pokhara University (PU) is an integration of information technology with management and business. BCA is a course of four years period which includes both technical and academic course. This program is designed to enhance student's business & managerial skills, problem analyzing & solving techniques...

We Provide

Discussion Forum

Our online forums can be used for many purposes, such as helping students to review material prior to an assignment or exam, engaging students in discussion of course material etc

Store

We provide marketplace for student to buy and sell their digital copy of BCA notes.This helps student to make some quick and easy cash while focusing on their study.

Entrance Preparation

Student can play a fun game that is solely created to help Students from various places of Nepal who are preparing for BCA entrance in Pokhara University affiliated colleges.

Notes

We provide simple and easy note downloading facilities for student of Pokhara University of numerous subject related to Bachelor of Computer Applicatiion.

Books

Student can download free e-books related to the course and view on their Computer. You can find both Textbook and Reference Book recommended by Pokhara University.

BCA Old Qustion and Syllabus

Here on web site, you can get all the Syllabus and Old Questions of BCA Pokhara University from first to eight Semester to help student prepare for the board exam.
